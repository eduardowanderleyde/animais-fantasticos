Fantastic Beasts

Fox

Foxes are omnivorous mammals belonging to the Canidae family. They are medium-sized vulpines characterized by a long snout and a long, bushy tail.

They also have oval-shaped pupils, similar to the vertical pupils of felines.

Squirrel

Squirrels are small rodents known for their bushy tails and climbing abilities.

Of about 200 recognized species of squirrels, many are found in forests around the world.

Bear

Bears are large mammals that inhabit various regions of the world, including North America, Asia, and Europe.

They are known for their strength and are omnivorous, feeding on a variety of foods, including fish, fruits, and plants.

Wolf

Wolves are carnivores belonging to the Canidae family and are known for living in social groups called packs.

They are skilled predators and play an important role in the ecosystems in which they live.

Monkey

Monkeys are primates found in various parts of the world and are known for their agility and intelligence.

They live in complex social groups and have a wide range of behaviors and characteristics, depending on the species.

Lion

Lions are large felines that primarily inhabit Africa. They live in groups known as "prides" and are powerful predators.

Lions are often regarded as the "kings of the jungle" due to their majesty and their position at the top of the food chain in their natural habitats.
